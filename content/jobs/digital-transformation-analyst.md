# Digital Transformation Analyst
**Operations & Digital Practice | Perth, Western Australia | Full-time**

## About the Role

Meridian Advisory is seeking a Digital Transformation Analyst to join our growing Operations & Digital Practice. Reporting directly to Partner Daniel Okafor, you'll play a crucial role in supporting our digital transformation engagements across mid-market and government clients throughout Western Australia.

This is an excellent opportunity for a recent IS or business graduate who's passionate about understanding how technology reshapes organisations. You'll work alongside our senior consultants on high-impact projects, gaining exposure to diverse industries including resources, government, and healthcare while developing your analytical and client-facing skills.

With our firm currently managing several significant digital transformation initiatives, including potential large-scale government programs, this role offers exceptional learning opportunities in a collaborative, partner-led environment.

## Key Responsibilities

- Conduct comprehensive process mapping and analysis to identify digital transformation opportunities
- Support requirements gathering workshops with client stakeholders across various organisational levels  
- Perform change impact assessments and develop recommendations for implementation approaches
- Assist in developing business cases and ROI analyses for digital initiatives
- Create detailed documentation and deliverables including process flows, requirements specifications, and implementation roadmaps
- Support data collection and analysis to inform transformation strategies
- Participate in client presentations and contribute to proposal development
- Collaborate with our 8-person Operations & Digital team on cross-functional project workstreams
- Research emerging technologies and best practices relevant to client industries

## What You'll Bring

- Bachelor's degree in Information Systems, Business, or related field with strong academic performance
- Demonstrated understanding of business processes and how technology enables organisational change
- Proficiency in process mapping tools (Visio, Lucidchart) and Microsoft Office Suite
- Strong analytical thinking with ability to synthesise complex information into clear insights
- Excellent written and verbal communication skills suitable for client-facing interactions
- Experience with project coordination or business analysis through internships, coursework, or part-time roles
- Interest in working across diverse industries and client environments
- Self-motivated with strong attention to detail and ability to manage multiple priorities
- Eligibility to work in Australia

**Preferred:**
- Exposure to change management frameworks or methodologies
- Understanding of agile project management principles
- Previous consulting or professional services experience

## What We Offer

- Competitive starting salary of $75,000 plus superannuation
- Direct mentorship from Partner Daniel Okafor and exposure to senior leadership across all engagements
- Comprehensive professional development including external training opportunities and conference attendance
- Clear career progression pathway within our expanding practice
- Collaborative team environment with only 45 employees — your contributions will be recognised and valued
- Flexible working arrangements including hybrid options
- Professional membership fees covered
- 4 weeks annual leave plus additional wellness days
- Health insurance contribution and employee assistance program

## How to Apply

Please submit your resume and a brief cover letter explaining your interest in digital transformation and what you'd bring to our team. Applications should be sent to careers@meridianadvisory.com.au with "Digital Transformation Analyst" in the subject line.

We review applications on a rolling basis and encourage early submission. Successful candidates will participate in a two-stage interview process including a case study presentation.

*Meridian Advisory is an equal opportunity employer committed to building a diverse and inclusive team.*
